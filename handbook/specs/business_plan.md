# Business Plan

## Revenue Model
- Licensing Fee (B2B): €25k–40k/year
- Free Tier (B2C): limited requests
- Licensed Museums: unlimited/enhanced requests

## Costs
- Infra fixed: ~€10k/year
- Variable: ~€0.07/request

## Revenue Projections
- Year 1: 5 museums → €165k
- Year 2: 20 museums → €660k
- Year 3: 50 museums → €1.65M
